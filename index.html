About

My name is Oscar Miñoso-Rendón...

And I am a Senior at Belen Jesuit Preparatory School in Miami, Florida with a keen interest in all things programming, philosophy, and theology.

As a peer minister and co-founder of the Order of Malta Youth Corps, I have always sought out ways to be of service to my community: recently working at Congresswoman Salazar's District Office as a Summer Intern.

When it comes to my aspirations for the future, I would like to one day work in cybersecurity or as a software engineer, making tools to aid people in their daily tasks.

When I am not studying, you can find me doing a number of things: drawing, reading, or working on Computer Related Projects with my friends, such as the Malta Youth Corps App, or Lacerta (the Goosebumps inspired game).
