Projects


The Malta Youth Corps App

The Malta Youth Corps App is a Progressive Web Application that is currently in the works and will aid me in administrating the Order of Malta Youth Corps by generating certificates of service to participating volunteers, and by helping me to stay in contact with them: announcing upcoming events and taking polls on expected attendance...

As the Order of Malta Meal Recollected Service's administrator, often collecting hundreds of meals a week, and having to award service hours to dozens of volunteers on a weekly basis, automating these tasks will greatly save me time.

The idea is also to make this application open source so that other NGOs and service organizations can fork it and take advantage of this application's tools as well!

Expected Date of Release: Late July.
